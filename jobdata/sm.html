Advertising and Promotion Head (Remote)

CodeBell PVT LTD


We have an exciting new opportunity for an Advertising and Promotion Head to join our team! This work-from-home position offers flexible hours and the chance to lead our social media and marketing efforts.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop, plan, and execute social media marketing campaigns across all major platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.).
  • Create engaging social media content, including posts, stories, videos, and ads that align with brand objectives.
  • Monitor and analyze campaign performance to optimize reach, engagement, and conversions.
  • Collaborate with designers, content creators, and other team members to ensure consistent messaging and branding.
  • Manage advertising budgets and allocate resources effectively for maximum ROI.
  • Research market trends, competitors, and audience insights to inform marketing strategies.
  • Prepare regular reports on campaign results and present recommendations for improvement.

Desired Skills & Qualifications

  • Proven experience in social media marketing, digital advertising, or promotion management.
  • Strong understanding of content creation, copywriting, and campaign planning.
  • Familiarity with social media management tools (e.g., Hootsuite, Buffer) and ad platforms (Facebook Ads Manager, Google Ads).
  • Creative mindset with the ability to develop innovative marketing ideas.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong analytical skills to interpret campaign data and make data-driven decisions.
  • Self-motivated and organized, with the ability to work independently in a remote, flexible-hours environment.

What We Offer

  • Fully remote work with flexible hours to fit your schedule.
  • Opportunity to lead and shape the company’s social media and advertising strategy.
  • A creative, supportive, and collaborative team environment.
  • Hands-on experience working on diverse campaigns across multiple platforms.
  • Competitive compensation with performance-based incentives.
  • Professional growth opportunities and the chance to make a real impact on our brand.
