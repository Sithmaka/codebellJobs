Marketing Executive (Remote / Hybrid)

CodeBell Advertising


We are looking for a creative, energetic, and driven Marketing Executive to join our growing team at CodeBell Advertising. In this role, you’ll lead the execution of digital and offline marketing campaigns, manage content calendars, and help bring our brand and client projects to life across multiple platforms.
If you’re passionate about social media, content creation, and campaign management - and want the flexibility to work remotely or in a hybrid setup - this opportunity is for you!

Key Responsibilities

  • Plan, execute, and manage digital marketing campaigns (social media, email, influencer, etc.).
  • Create engaging content and coordinate content calendars across platforms like Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and TikTok.
  • Manage day-to-day social media activity including posting, responding to messages/comments, and community engagement.
  • Collaborate with designers, writers, and video editors to develop campaign content.
  • Monitor campaign performance and create reports using insights from Meta, Google, and analytics tools.
  • Assist in client meetings, brainstorming sessions, and content planning.
  • Stay updated with the latest trends in digital marketing, social media, and branding.

Desired Skills & Qualifications

  • Background in Marketing, Communication, Digital Media, or a related field.
  • Experience with social media management tools (e.g., Meta Business Suite, Buffer, Hootsuite).
  • Strong creativity, writing, and storytelling skills.
  • Familiarity with basic graphic tools like Canva, and bonus if you know Adobe tools.
  • Comfortable managing multiple tasks and working in a deadline-driven environment.
  • Proactive, detail-oriented, and a great communicator.
  • Experience in running or supporting paid ad campaigns is a plus.

What We Offer

  • Remote or hybrid working flexibility.
  • A fun, fast-paced, and collaborative creative environment.
  • Opportunities to work on real marketing campaigns for CodeBell and its clients.
  • Hands-on experience with a supportive team and mentoring.
  • Performance-based incentives and career growth opportunities.
  • A chance to lead campaigns and make real impact early in your career.
