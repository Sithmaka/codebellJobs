Quality Assurance (QA) Intern / Engineer (Remote / Hybrid)

CodeBell PVT LTD


At CodeBell, quality is at the core of everything we build. We’re looking for a detail-oriented and analytical QA Intern or Engineer to help ensure the reliability, performance, and user experience of our web and software products. You’ll work closely with developers, designers, and product managers to identify bugs, optimize performance, and maintain top-notch standards.
Whether you’re an aspiring QA intern or an early-career engineer, if you’re passionate about testing and quality - we want you on our team!

Key Responsibilities

  • Create, maintain, and execute test plans and test cases.
  • Perform manual and automated testing on web/mobile applications.
  • Identify, document, and report bugs and issues clearly.
  • Collaborate with developers and designers to ensure issues are resolved.
  • Conduct regression testing before releases.
  • Participate in UI/UX reviews to provide quality feedback.
  • Ensure software meets both functional and non-functional requirements.

Desired Skills & Qualifications

  • Basic understanding of software development lifecycle and QA processes.
  • Familiarity with testing types: functional, usability, performance, regression.
  • Experience with tools like Jira, Postman, Selenium, or Cypress is a plus.
  • Ability to think critically and spot inconsistencies or edge cases.
  • Strong attention to detail and written communication.
  • Eagerness to learn and grow in a remote or hybrid working environment.

What We Offer

  • Remote or hybrid working model with flexible hours.
  • Exposure to real-world QA workflows and tools.
  • A collaborative environment with mentorship from experienced developers.
  • Opportunity to contribute to live client projects.
  • Performance-based rewards and future full-time opportunity.
  • Skill development in automation testing, bug tracking, and test planning.
