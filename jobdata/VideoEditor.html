Video Editor (Remote / Hybrid)

CodeBell Advertising


CodeBell Advertising is on the lookout for a creative and detail-oriented Video Editor to craft compelling visual stories across our digital platforms. From brand reels and social media content to promo videos and campaign teasers, you’ll be responsible for shaping how brands come to life on screen.
If you’re passionate about content creation, have an eye for detail, and love bringing raw footage into a polished final product—this is your calling. Bonus if you’re TikTok-savvy and Instagram Reel-obsessed!

Key Responsibilities

  • Edit and produce engaging videos for social media, ads, branding, and client campaigns.
  • Collaborate with the creative team to bring storyboards and scripts to life.
  • Add motion graphics, subtitles, sound effects, transitions, and color grading as needed.
  • Manage and organize raw footage, maintain video libraries, and follow branding guidelines.
  • Stay updated with trends on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
  • Repurpose long-form videos into snackable short-form content.
  • Meet project deadlines and revise content based on feedback.

Desired Skills & Qualifications

  • Proficiency in video editing software such as Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Final Cut Pro, or CapCut.
  • Basic knowledge of motion graphics and sound design.
  • Strong storytelling sense and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work independently in a remote/hybrid setup.
  • Creativity, problem-solving skills, and a passion for video content.
  • Portfolio or links to previous work are highly preferred.

What We Offer

  • Remote or hybrid work with flexible hours.
  • Creative freedom to work on exciting brand and client projects.
  • A collaborative team culture where your ideas matter.
  • Opportunity to grow your portfolio with real-world, high-visibility content.
  • Performance-based bonuses and full-time opportunities.
