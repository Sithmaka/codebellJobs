Strategy Planner (Remote / Hybrid)

CodeBell Advertising


We’re looking for a strategic thinker with a creative edge to join us as a Strategy Planner. In this role, you’ll shape compelling marketing strategies that connect brands to the right audience and deliver measurable results.
This is your chance to work with a young, vibrant, and forward-thinking team - from anywhere, with flexible working hours.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop data-driven marketing strategies for clients and internal projects.
  • Research target audiences, competitors, trends, and consumer behavior to guide campaign development.
  • Work closely with the creative and marketing teams to ensure alignment of brand messaging and goals.
  • Create compelling briefs and strategy decks for internal and client presentations.
  • Collaborate with content creators, designers, and media planners to ensure strategy execution.
  • Monitor campaign performance and recommend improvements based on insights and analytics.
  • Stay up to date with digital trends, tools, and innovations in advertising and marketing.

Desired Skills & Qualifications

  • A background in Marketing, Advertising, Business, Communication, or related fields.
  • Strong analytical and critical thinking skills – able to turn data into insights and ideas.
  • Understanding of digital marketing platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.).
  • Experience in using tools like Google Trends, Meta Ads Library, competitor research tools, or similar.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Creative mindset with strategic execution.
  • Ability to manage multiple campaigns in a remote and fast-paced environment.

What We Offer

  • A creative, supportive, and collaborative team environment.
